Model X Features

The Model X is a mid-size, all-electric luxury SUV that boasts impressive performance and advanced features such as:

  • 0-60 mph in as little as 2.5 seconds
  • Range of up to 371 miles on a single charge
  • Falcon Wing doors for easy entry and exit
  • Autopilot advanced safety and convenience features

Overall, the Model X is a stunning example of Tesla's commitment to innovation and sustainability.
